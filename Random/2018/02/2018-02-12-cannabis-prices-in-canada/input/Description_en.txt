


Crowdsourced data on dried cannabis prices, use, and consumption

Observations in the data represent self-reported information submitted by website visitors concerning previous purchases of dried cannabis.
The data contain no identifiable information as all submissions are done anonymously.

Source: Statistics Canada, Crowdsourcing Cannabis (https://surveys-enquetes.statcan.gc.ca/cannabis/en)
Geography: Census subdivision
Reference period: The application was launched January 25, 2018. Data are being collected continuously and will be made available as new information comes in.

Official Statistics Canada estimates for cannabis prices in Canada can be found on CANSIM Table 388-0011.
For more information on cannabis prices and related information, please consult the Cannabis Stats Hub at https://www.statcan.gc.ca/pub/13-610-x/13-610-x2018001-eng.htm.
